Let's make a beautiful triangle!

Equilateral!

All of your sides are equal so you have an equilateral, my friend!

Isosceles!

Two your sides are equal so you have an isosceles, my friend!

Scalene!

None of your sides are equal so you have a scalene, my friend!

You don't have a triangle...

The sum of the lengths of any of your sides are less than or equal to the length of your third side so you don't have a triangle, my friend. But, you do have a horse.
